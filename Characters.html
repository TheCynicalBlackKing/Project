Characters

Most Cybertronians can be grouped into three categories, neutral (taking neither side), Autobot (or either hero-like equivalent in the series), or Decepticons (or other villain-like equivalent in the series). Although there is not much that differs between the three groups other than ideology, it is not uncommon for a Transformer to change sides, or to take a vow of neutrality. The few things that set apart the various groups tend to be their alternate modes (although this may not always be the case, but each faction tends to share a common theme), what their goals are (protect, destry, etc).
